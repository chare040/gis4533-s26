Three Points of Interest in Duluth

by: Alexander Charette

This map highlights three of my favorite places around Duluth. The Lake Superior Railroad Museum showcases one of the largest collections of historic locomotives in the country, making it a must‑see for anyone interested in the region’s rail history. Marcus Duluth Cinema is my go‑to spot for catching new movies with comfortable recliner seating. Finally, the iconic Aerial Lift Bridge stands as one of Duluth’s most recognizable landmarks, rising dozens of times each day to let ships pass through the canal. Together, these locations capture a mix of history, entertainment, and local character that make Duluth such a unique place.
